Description

Kanheri Caves are a group of rock-cut monuments located in the forests of the Sanjay Gandhi National Park, on the outskirts of Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. These caves date back to the 1st century BCE and served as Buddhist monastic complexes. They feature intricately carved sculptures, reliefs, and pillars, showcasing ancient Buddhist art and architecture.
