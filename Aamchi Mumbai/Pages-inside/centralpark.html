Description

Discover Thane's expansive Grand Central Park, spanning over 20 acres and adorned with a lush canopy of 4,500+ native trees. Immerse yourself in the beauty of nature within this urban oasis, featuring a generous 3-acre lake that complements the topography, creating a microclimate to counteract the urban heat island effect. In the midst of a concrete-dominated cityscape, it is our collective responsibility as stewards of the Earth to replenish and nurture the natural resources we utilize. Embracing a Biophilic Design approach, the Kalpataru Parkcity township in Thane seamlessly integrates with nature throughout its development, with Grand Central Park at its core. This verdant haven stands out as Thane's Newest Green Lung, boasting an impressive 95% open space. Step into Grand Central Park, where the synergy of thoughtful planning and natural harmony welcomes you to a rejuvenating escape in the heart of the city.the NaMo Grand Central Park, spread across 20 acres, is a gift of greens to Thane amidst the greys of concrete.It is one of the most well-planned natural spaces.
