Local Fast Food

Explore Mumbai's local fast food delights

Vada Pav

The iconic Mumbai street food, spicy potato fritter in a bun.

Misal Pav

Spicy sprouted lentils curry served with pav (bread).

Pav Bhaji

Buttered bread served with spicy mashed vegetables.

Pani Puri

Crispy puris filled with spicy, tangy water, tamarind chutney, and a mix of potatoes and chickpeas.

Bhel Puri

A tangy and spicy mixture of puffed rice, vegetables, and tamarind sauce, garnished with sev.

Dabeli

Spicy mashed potatoes stuffed in a bun, garnished with pomegranate, peanuts, and sev
